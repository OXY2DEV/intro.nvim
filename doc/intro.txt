The most notable features of the plugin are,
    - Automatic `vertical` alignment of lines.
    - Various `horizontal` alignment options(left, center, right) for texts.
    - Ability to color parts of the texts precisely without knowledge of
      `byte-index`.
    - Gradient colors.
    - Animations.
And many more minor features!

`â”ƒ NOTE`
`â”ƒ` This plugin was made entirely inside `Termux` and was originally intended for
`â”ƒ` usage in `Termux`.
`â”ƒ` As such, somethings may get a bit buggy on `Desktops`.
 
`â”ƒ INFORMATION`
`â”ƒ` If you have encountered a `bug`, you should first check if the bug has already
`â”ƒ` been reported or not.
`â”ƒ` When creating a bug report, please try to provide information about your
`â”ƒ` terminal, neovim version, what actually happened etc.
`â”ƒ` You may also attach a short video if you can.
